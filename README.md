# House of Bijou — Building Freedom Through Technology

> Autonomous digital infrastructure for black African queer females, trans and gender-queer folks, and survivors—enabling safety, economic empowerment, and authentic community without surveillance.

---

## Overview

House of Bijou is a **stealth-by-design** digital platform built for and by black African queer females, trans and gender-queer people, and survivors of sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV) in Kenya and East Africa.

The app masquerades as a benign utility (calculator, weather app, or game) while providing layered safety, community, and economic tools to users who face intersecting threats of surveillance, violence, and financial exclusion.

**Live Site:** [https://tich-labs.github.io/houseofbijou-d-app/](https://tich-labs.github.io/houseofbijou-d-app/)

---

## The Problem

In Kenya and East Africa, community members face:

- **Digital Tracking & Control** — Ex-partners and family members use mobile network location services to monitor movements, snoop on phones, and weaponize screenshots for blackmail and outing.
- **Banking Exclusion** — Traditional financial systems are inaccessible or unsafe for many community members.
- **Criminalization & Custody Threats** — Sexuality, gender identity, and survival work are criminalized. Queer parents face custody threats. Police involvement looms if identities are exposed.
- **Erasure & Inauthenticity** — Existing digital spaces do not center the lived realities of this community.

*These are not hypothetical problems. They are the daily reality for our community.*

## Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Stealth Mode** | Launches as a calculator, weather app, or game. A secret gesture/PIN reveals the real community dashboard. If your phone is checked, no one knows what's inside. |
| **Panic Alert** | One shake, gesture, or button press silently alerts 3–5 trusted contacts with your location. Community emergency responders mobilize in minutes. |
| **Mutual Check-Ins** | A 3-person tracking system. You keep tabs on peers when moving around; they keep tabs on you. No central surveillance—pure peer protection. |
| **Encrypted Chat** | End-to-end encrypted messages with delete-on-timer. No server logs. Discuss safety, resistance strategies, and chosen family with zero traces. |
| **Low-Trace Transfers** | Send and receive money peer-to-peer without a bank account. Works on feature phones via USSD. Encrypted ledger. Hard to freeze, hard to trace. |
| **Mutual Aid** | Transparent community resource pooling. Post a request for emergency funds; community responds within 24 hours. Radical solidarity, recorded trust. |

## Tech Stack

| Technology | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Rails + PostgreSQL** | Fast iteration, built-in encryption, real-time capability. One developer can ship features in days, not months. |
| **Hotwire + Tailwind CSS** | Low bandwidth, accessible, works on slow connections. 70% less data than React. |
| **Turbo Native** | One Rails app = web + Android + iOS. Instant updates, no App Store delays. Security fixes deploy in minutes. |
| **USSD + SMS** | Works on any phone, any connectivity. Feature phone users, offline users—all included. |
| **AES-256 + Signal Protocol** | Everything sensitive is encrypted. No cloud logging. Even if servers are hacked, user data stays safe. |
| **Local-First Storage** | All sensitive data stays on your device. Synced to server only if you want. Maximum control. |

> **Infrastructure cost:** ~$50–100/month. Scales to 50,000+ users without significant increase.

## Economic Empowerment Model

Safety without economic independence is incomplete. House of Bijou is designed to build economic power at every stage.

| Tier | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Emergency Survival | Community panic fund (0% interest emergency loans). Rapid mutual aid with 24-hour mobilization. Survive immediate crisis without exploitation. |
| **2** | Sustainable Income | Low-trace peer transfers. Transparent mutual aid ledger that builds community credit history. Survival income resources (gig work, crafts) managed safely. |
| **3** | Economic Autonomy | App ledger as proof of income for microloans. Financial literacy workshops. Blockchain wallet (Year 2) for censorship-resistant funds. |
| **4** | Collective Power | Track aggregate resource flows for movement-building and policy advocacy. Build cooperative/social enterprise from community fund. |

**Revenue model:** A 1–2% peer transfer fee funds hosting, USSD, and moderation—community wealth-building, not extraction.

## Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP (Months 1–4)
- Stealth mode, panic button, encrypted local storage, values-based onboarding, basic encrypted chat
- **Users:** 5–10 community members (invite-only beta)
- **Budget:** $30k development · $6k infrastructure · $8k security audit · $18k community coordination

### Phase 2: Mobile & Scale (Months 5–8)
- Turbo Native Android app, biometric unlock, location privacy features, money tools beta, USSD integration
- **Users:** 50–100 active users, 3–5 community partnerships
- **Funding:** 60% grants · 40% early transfer fees + ally subscriptions

### Phase 3: Ecosystem & Sustainability (Months 9–12)
- Blockchain integration (stablecoin wallet), chosen family narrative features, accessibility audit, community governance structure
- **Users:** 100–500 active users, sustainable funding model active
- **Goal:** 80%+ self-funded by year-end
